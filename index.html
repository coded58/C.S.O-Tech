SERVICES

Due to our vast experience and knowledge in this industry in Nigeria, we have successfully undertaken both big and small projects in the following categories:

ARC WELDING

This is a welding process used to join metal to metal by using electricity to create enough heat to melt metal, and the melted metals, when cool, result in a binding of the metal

TIG/ARGON WEILDING

Popularly known as tungsten inert gas (TIG) wielding, is a wielding process that uses non-consumable tungsten electrode to produce the wield

GAS METAL WEILDING

Sometimes referred to by its subtypes metal inert gas (MIG) and metal active gas (MAG) is a wielding process in which a electric arc forms between a consumable MIG wire electrode and the workpiece metal(s), which heats the workpiece metal(s), causing them to fuse
